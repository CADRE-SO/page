CADRE STUDENT ORGANIZATION

The Cadre Student Organization(SO) was founded in August of 2005 at San Jose State University. It consists of a collective of students who are exploring the intersection of art, design, and technology. The organization operates out of San Jose States Cadre Media Lab, working as both artist and engineers.

Cadre SO offers workshops for soft and technical skills, host fun social activities like paint night and game night, and invite artists and DMA Alumni to guest speaker events.

As Digital Media Artists and club members, we create works based on the use of digital video, physical computing, interactive design, installation art, web development and game studies. We hope to share our interests in creating a multidisciplinary platform, and showcasing new ideas with the matriculated student body.
