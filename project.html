CAFE AFTERLIFE 2019

Description:
Café Afterlife is a reality-bending installation that brings a conjuration of ghosts, ghouls, and spirits on Día de Muertos! A collaborative project fabricated by the San José State University CADRE Student Organization(CSO) held at the San José Hammer Theatre as part of San José's First Friday community events in downtown. Inspired and influenced by the themes of Día de Muertos(Day of the Dead), the CSO transformed the Hammer Theatre's café space to a portal to an alternative world. The world of the living and the afterlife converging together. Spirits haunt the walls by possessing the paintings along the wall, a ghostly bartender appears to offer you a drink, souls manifesting from the spectral portal to our world. During the spiritual event, specially crafted treats and goodies were offered to quell the howls of the night. https://www.southfirstfridays.com/2019/10/november-1-2019-south-first-fridays-art-walk/

The collaborative student body used a mix of practical and digital effects to manifest the portal to Café Afterlife. Interdisciplinary techniques of set design, 2D and 3D animation, video editing, creative coding, electronics, and more to bring the space alive.

Programs used but not limited to, MadMapper, TouchDesigner, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects, Unity, Maya, and Blender.

Special thank you to the SJSU Hammer Theatre, CADRE Labs, SJSU Art Department, and the Theatre Department for the support and rental equipment.

CADRE ZONE 2018

Description:
You have entered into the Cadre Zone! A collaborative installation by San José State University CADRE Student Organization (CSO) held in Room 237 of the art building. This sci-fi, mind bending walkthrough featured projection mapping, fabricated arduino sculptures and a VR experience. Inspired by The Twilight Zone, CSO blurs the boundaries between reality and fantasy. Have your fortune told by our fortune teller fabricated with cardboard and styrofoam and programmed using arduinos. Engage yourself in portals that take you into virtual worlds filled with horror and absurdity. Become immersed by televisions filled with macabre symbols that encapsulate the horrifying and the uncanny.
